Wrapped PixelCats

if you wish to unwrap your pixelcats, you can do so here
coming soon! if you really need to unwrap your cats, use the etherscan link below
and call unwrap() with an array of the ids you wish to unwrap
(for example, unwrapping ids 1 through 3 would be [1,2,3])
